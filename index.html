Types of Stones

20MM

20MM is mainly used to construct homes And also in construction of roads

WMM

Wmm means water mixed material its a mixture of Dust, 4mm, 6mm, 10mm, 20mm, and 40mm used to construct roads and pool and also used to contruct petrolpump. maximum size of stone not should be more than 40mm

Dust & 4MM

Dust is used mainly used to construct homes and also used to attach tiles in homes and 4mm is also used for this purpose and dust and 4mm also used to by damar plant to make damar which is used to make roads

6MM

6mm is used to attach tiles and pedestral and used by damar plant to make damar.

10MM

10mm is used in construction of homes and also used to lay in front of homes and also used by damar plant to make damar.

JSB

JSB is used to make roads and also used by petrolpump jsb is mixture of all stones but in that maximum size should be not more than 60mm and we can also used muram to make jsb but quantity of muram should not be more than 25 percentage
